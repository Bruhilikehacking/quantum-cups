📘 Story

In the neon depths of the Quantum Realm, unstable atoms drift through the void. Photons — pure packets of energy — flicker between orbitals, while rogue electrons threaten to destabilize everything they touch.

You are the Quantum Operator, tasked with restoring balance by capturing photons, avoiding electrons, and stabilizing atoms using your knowledge of chemistry. Unlock elemental skins, master quantum powerups, and rise through the atomic ranks.

🎮 How to Play

  • Click an atom to reveal what’s inside.
  • Find the photon to earn money.
  • Avoid the electron unless you have a shield.
  • Use money to unlock elemental skins — each gives bonuses.
  • Answer chemistry questions to activate powerups.
  • Photon Burst = double money.
  • Electron Void = no electrons for one round.
  • Atom Swap = swap two atoms before choosing.
  • Quantum Peek = see where the photon is.
